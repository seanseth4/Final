  VFX

Visual effects are very powerful in how they have the ability to enhance visuals and even messages. In my experience with this assignment, using Adobe After Effects was challenging but was certainly fun. I tried to follow one tutorial and it was a little too hard for me, so I chose another and created the "fire" effect. To create the effect, I used a total of 4 layers, and I had to toggle the settings (such as the birth size, resistance, etc) and positioning of all of them to create the movement. There is no "setting" necessarily to create the appearance of fire, so what was done was using a variety of fast moving particles. I added the turbulant displace effect to make it move even more randomly (like fire does), and then used color correction properties to give it it's final appearance.

New Technologies Challenges

Games

According to studies that have been done, I learned that gaming can be used to solve world problems, like hunger, homelessness, etc. When playing games, gamers tend to have extreme concentration and hopefulness; the goal that researchers in this field have is to transfer that motivation into real life. For example, in a story-based game, each mission given to a player is given when they have the skillset to achieve it. This lets gamers feel motivated because they know they can accomplish something. Meanwhile in real life, sometimes we are given tasks that we may or may not be able to accomplish, which leaves us feeling discouraged, and the goal is to change that. Gamers are getting good at urgent optimism (urge to act immediately to solve something), social fabric (bonding), blissful productivity (motivation to work hard), and desire for epic meaning (gamers enjoy working towards a big goal like saving the world), making them super-empowered hopeful individuals. Gamers believe they can change an online world in their games, and the goal is to move that inspiration to real life. Ever since studies have been made on this, games have been developed to actually have real-world effects, such as an online game called "World Without Oil", that teaches you skill sets that can help you in real life with saving money, and having healthier habits that will help the environment.

Deepfakes

Deepfakes have pros and cons depending on how they're used. When it comes to the pros, using deepfakes can help enhance education, by creating models of past figures, like Albert Einstein, and have him teach the theory of relativity. This could make educational environments more engaging and fun for students, which is great! Unfortunately, when dealing with deepfakes, there is a lot of repsonsibility because it can be easily misused as well. Deepfakes can and have been used to impersonate people, especially political figures and other popular social figures, and have them say things they haven't. It is hard for the average person to identify if what video they're watching is fake or not, so this is incredibly detrimental in the spreading of false information and can unjustly change the perception of people. Not only that, but any person, celebrity or not, can be targeted, such as random girls getting their selfies stolen and used on deepfake porn moodels. While there are pros and cons, as of right now, the negatives of deepfakes seem to be more popular and in use.

AR/VR

The future and even the present of Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) technology is incredibly promising. AR and VR allow the spreading of information in more accessible ways and more interactive ways. For example, VR technology can be used in educational environments like a chem lab, and students can conduct their experiments virtually instead, which would save them money on having to buy equipment to actually conduct the experiment in real life. AR and VR can also be used to conduct real life training scenarios for people in professions like fire fighter training, etc, or surgeons can use VR to simulate a surgery before performing it, to guarentee it's success. There are so many other uses of AR and VR that can have many benefits, and even can be used just for fun and enhancing gaming experiences. Overall, AR and VR technology is definietly useful for humanity.
